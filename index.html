Executive Summary

AWV Telehealth is an AI-first, audio-only telemedicine platform dedicated to Medicare Annual Wellness Visits (AWVs). Utilizing advanced real-time AI voice engines, we enhance provider efficiency, expand patient access—especially in underserved and rural areas—and prioritize responsibility and compassionate care. Positioned as an attractive acquisition target for major healthcare providers and private equity firms, AWV Telehealth is set to transform preventive healthcare for millions of Medicare beneficiaries.

Problem Statement

  • Provider Burden: Time-intensive AWVs lead to revenue losses and CMS penalties.
  • Limited Patient Access: Barriers in rural and underserved areas result in unmet preventive care needs.
  • Underutilization of AWVs: Low completion rates hinder early detection and increase long-term healthcare costs.
  • Digital Divide: Lack of access to video-based telehealth due to low broadband, technological proficiency, or language barriers.

Our Solution

AWV Telehealth addresses these challenges with a comprehensive, AI-driven approach:

  • AI-Enhanced Remote AWVs: Conduct Medicare-compliant, audio-only visits via advanced AI voice engines, maximizing accessibility without in-person appointments.
  • Dedicated Nurse and AI Hybrid Network: Combine trained nurses with AI support for efficient, high-quality AWVs, ensuring consistency and personalized care.
  • Advanced Automation and Data Collection: Streamline scheduling, documentation, billing, and data collection through AI-driven processes, reducing administrative burdens.
  • SDOH Integration: Incorporate Social Determinants of Health (SDOH) assessments to identify and address broader patient needs, connecting them with community resources.
